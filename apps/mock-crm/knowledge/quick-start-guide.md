## Welcome to Acme CRM!

Acme CRM helps you manage contacts, track deals, and grow your revenue. Here's how to get started in 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Explore the Dashboard

When you first log in, you'll land on the **Dashboard** (`index.html`).

**What you'll see:**
- **Stats cards** at the top showing your key metrics (contacts, deals, revenue, email rate)
- **Revenue chart** on the left showing monthly trends
- **Activity feed** on the right showing what your team has been doing
- **Top deals table** at the bottom showing your most valuable pipeline items

**Try this**: Look at the stats cards to get an overview of your business metrics.

---

## Step 2: Add Your First Contact

1. Click **"Contacts"** in the left sidebar (üë• icon)
2. Click the purple **"+ Add Contact"** button in the top-right corner
3. Fill in the contact's details:
   - Name (required)
   - Email (required)
   - Phone
   - Company
   - Role/Title
4. Click **"Save Contact"**

**Pro tip**: You can also bulk import contacts by clicking the **"Import"** button and uploading a CSV file.

---

## Step 3: Create Your First Deal

1. Click **"Deals"** in the left sidebar (ü§ù icon)
2. You'll see a **Kanban board** with columns for each pipeline stage
3. Click the purple **"+ New Deal"** button in the top-right
4. Enter deal details:
   - Deal name (e.g., "Acme Corp Enterprise License")
   - Value (e.g., $48,000)
   - Contact (link to an existing contact)
   - Stage (starts in "Lead")
5. Click **"Create Deal"**

**Moving deals**: Drag deal cards between columns to update their stage, or click a card and change the stage dropdown.

---

## Step 4: Check Your Reports

1. Click **"Reports"** in the left sidebar (üìà icon)
2. Select a date range from the dropdown (top-right)
3. Click **"Generate Report"** (purple button)
4. View your KPIs, revenue trend, and pipeline breakdown
5. To export: click the **"üì• Export PDF"** button in the header

## Step 5: Review Your Billing

1. Click **"Billing"** in the left sidebar (üí≥ icon, under "Settings")

4. View your invoice history at the bottom
